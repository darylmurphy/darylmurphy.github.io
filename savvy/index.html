Welcome to Savvy

Daryl Murphy needs no introduction.

His stylistic versatility, incredible vocal range and zest for being on stage are world renowned. Daryl's performance credits include working with Keith Urban, The Veronicas and Powderfinger.

Combine this with five of Brisbane's most accomplished musicians and you have an energetic, fun and professional outfit which will captivate any audience.

Having performed at hundreds of major corporate events, the members of Savvy consistently deliver the complete entertainment package.

A Wealth of Experience

Under the musical direction of Toby Loveland, this accomplished band consists of musicians at the top of their field.

No matter what the event, this selection of skilled performers appeals to all audiences and are adept at playing for every occasion.

Of course it's not all about sounding good. Visually the Savvy band delivers an energy and enthusiasm unsurpassed.

Savvy is all this, and more.

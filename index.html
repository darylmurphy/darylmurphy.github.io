About

Daryl Murphy is a successful singer/songwriter and without doubt one of Australia's most heard vocalists, lending his vocal talent to over 1500 television and radio jingles.

At home or on the international stage, Daryl has performed at hundreds of major corporate events. He also recorded the theme song for the hit BBC series Misery Guts, and his original songs can be heard on the US TV series The New Adventures of Flipper.

Unless you've been marooned on a desert island you would've heard Daryl's voice. Don't miss the opportunity to see this talented performer in action!

On the local front, he heads up the successful corporate functions band Savvy, and the equally engaging duo/trio, Phoenix. Both outfits combine the incredible talents of guitarist Toby Loveland, with Daryl's energy and immense four octave range.

The "Brisbane Cabaret Festival" has also provided a platform to showcase Daryl's creative talents where one of the many successful performances took place in the world famous "Speigeltent".
